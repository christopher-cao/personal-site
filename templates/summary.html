Hong Kong

Hong Kong University of Science and Technology | 2015-2016

Having discovered my interest in computer science, I sought out opportunities at HKUST to further develop my technical skills.

I joined the university robotics team, where I had to make the difficult transition from developing in Python to C. Along with developing software in C and, eventually Python as well, I was elected to lead a team of 13 engineering students (software, mechanical, and electrical) from 4 nationalities to build a robot from scratch and compete in HKUST’s Robot Design competition. As a Microsoft Student Partner, I also gained experience as a computer science instructor, developing and teaching a curriculum to pre-collegiate students in Hong Kong.

Bangkok, Thailand

While interning as a business analyst for Indochina Healthcare in Bangkok, I also had the opportunity to compete in my first hackathon: AngelHack Thailand 2016. Milan, Italy

Bocconi University | 2016-2017

While studying at Bocconi University, I had the opportunity to apply what I learned from the summer as a student researcher for the Bocconi Institute for Data Science and Analytics. Seattle, Washington

While working at the Boeing Company as a project management intern, I developed a full-stack web application that automates the creation and revision of production schedules to support the development of the 787 Dreamliner.
